    What is Emotional Intelligence?

    Eq is being able to understand, balance your own emotions in positive ways to relieve stress, communicate effectively, empathize with others, overcome challenges and defuse conflict.

    How is it different to IQ?

    IQ is your ability to solve problems, use logic, or communicate big ideas. EQ tests your ability to recognize emotion in yourself and others, and to use that awareness to help make clear decisions.
